# GlowOS Finance - Launch Copy

## Product Hunt 🚀

**Tagline:** GlowOS Finance - The AI-driven, privacy-first Mint alternative.

**Description:**
Say goodbye to endless manual budgeting and complex spreadsheets! With Mint shutting down, we built **GlowOS Finance** to help you take control of your financial future effortlessly. 

GlowOS Finance is a modern, automated personal finance platform powered by AI. 

✨ **Features:**
- **Smart AI Categorization:** No more endless regex rules. Our AI securely and intelligently categorizes your transactions.
- **Automated Statement Parsing:** Seamlessly extract and clean your bank PDF and CSV statements.
- **Beautiful Dashboard Analytics:** Understand your cash flow, spending, and budget adherence at a glance.
- **Privacy First:** Your financial data stays secure. 

Whether you're managing daily expenses or planning for the future, GlowOS Finance works for you. Try it today and let us know what you think! 👇
